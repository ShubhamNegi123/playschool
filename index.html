PROBLEM THAT PLAY SCHOOL ARE FACING IN DESIGNING REQUIREMENTS?

Play schools are mostly located in residential areas or inside the localities. It makes them out of reach with the professional and quality companies that are proving designing & printing services. Play schools are facing various problems in designing of their various and regular requirements. Few of points as below:

  • Costing on higher side if hired good and effective designing companies
  • Lack of accuracy and concept in designing with printer side design creations
  • Have to deal with various vendors of different requirements. It makes their professional life miserable
  • Lots of time wastage
  • Lack of professionalism and industry knowledge in local vendors
  • Difficulty in marketing contacts etc.
